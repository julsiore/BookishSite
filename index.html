My Favorite Books

As someone who talks about books on the internet, I have read many. These are my favorites.

  1. Northanger Abbey by Jane Austen
  2. The Picture of Dorian Gray by Oscar Wilde
  3. The Illustrated Man by Ray Bradbury
  4. Carmilla by Sheridan Le Fanu

The perfect reading moment

For me, a perfect reading moment includes a cup of coffee, a comfy place to sit and a great book. It is important for me to look at reviews before reading a book...


Where to find book reviews.

For book reviews, I prefer the website Goodreads, it lets you read opinions from lots of users, and you can follow your favorite reviewers!


My favorite author

The first book I mentioned was Northanger Abbey. It was written by Jane Austen, my favorite writer. These are some facts about her:

  • She was born in Steventon, United Kingdom, in 1775.
  • She wrote six novels in total.
  • Most of her books have been adapted for movies, like Pride and Prejudice in 2005.
